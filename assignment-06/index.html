DIY Snow Globe Tutorial



Materials

  • Small glass jar
  • Plastic figurine/toy
  • Glycerin (optional: helps glitter float)
  • Glitter
  • Sequins
  • Water (optional: add food coloring for more fun!)
  • Spoon
  • Hot glue gun (Tip: if you don't have a hot glue gun, use super glue)


Directions

  1. Find a small figurine or toy to be the focal point of the snow globe
  2. Put glue on the inside of the jar's lid and stick figurine/toy to it
  3. Fill the jar with cold water
  4. Add 1-2 tsp. of glitter and stir
  5. Optional: if using Glycerin, add a few drops now

  6. Screw lid on jar
  7. That's it! Shake the jar or turn it upside down to see it "snow!"
