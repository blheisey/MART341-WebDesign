How to Plan a Vacation

Instructable

What you'll need:

  • Information source
    • Electronic device(computer, phone, tablet, etc.)
    • Travel book about your destination
  • Place for Taking Notes
    • Google Doc or Word Document
    • Notebook

Step 1: Explore the map for travel destinations

Not sure where to travel? Check out this article on the best places to travel in 2023

Step 2: Transportation

  • If flying:
    • Book your plane ticket ASAP
    • Make sure passport is up to date if traveling internationally
    • Arrange transportation to the airport
  • If driving:
    • Make sure your vehicle is in good condition for the trip
    • Make sure passport is up to date if traveling internationally
    • Plan your route

Step 3: Plan Your Itinerary

  • Use your chosen research method to find restaurants, attractions, and activities
  • Create your itineray in your chosen place for notes
    • Make a section for each day of your vacation
    • Under each day, include times and locations of events
    • Add a section for notes about each day to include additional information

Step 4: Packing

  • Make packing lists for each of your bags: personal bag, carry-on, check-on(if you have one)
  • Pack anything you can the day before your trip
  • Make a list of items that can't be packed until the day you leave

Step 5: Leave for Your Vacation

  • Make sure you have all your belongings
  • Have a great trip!
